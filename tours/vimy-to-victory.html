Vimy to Victory Day Tour

Canada's greatest battles in the greatest war ever fought.

Home / Tours / Vimy to Victory

Follow the trail from Vimy to victory during an unforgettable day on the battlefields of the Artois! Explore the preserved trenches, tunnels, and battlefield of Vimy Ridge before visiting the iconic Canadian National Vimy Memorial, one of Canada's most important national monuments.

After lunch, continue to the battlefields of Hill 70, Monchy-le-Preux, and the Hundred Days Offensive where Canadian and Newfoundland soldiers achieved some of the most remarkable victories of the war. Along the way, visit both iconic and lesser-known battlefield locations that bring you into the footsteps of soldiers and reveal the full and heroic story of Canada's contribution in the First World War.

Morning

  • Canadian Corps battle headquarters
  • Frontline trenches and underground tunnels on Vimy Ridge
  • Vimy Ridge battlefield and mine craters
  • Canadian National Vimy Memorial
Lunch is taken near Vimy.

Afternoon

  • Battle of Hill 70 Canadian battlefield & Memorial Park
  • Monchy-le-Preux battlefield and the Newfoundland Memorial
  • Drocourt-Quéant Line Canadian battlefield and Memorial at Dury
  • Canal du Nord battlefield (time permitting)

The above itinerary may be varied based on the wishes and consensus of the guests travelling on any particular day. This tour is also included within The Signature (2 Day) and The Ultimate (3 Day) Canadian Tours.
